JOLLOF SPAGHETTI

INGREDIENTS DETAILS
Pasta: Spaghetti na!!
Red bell peppers, tomatoes, scotch bonnet, and onions: for making the sauce. The habanero brings heat and spiciness to the sauce.
If you’re not a fan of spicy food, you can use less or omit it altogether.
Onions, garlic, and fresh ginger: add depth to the sauce
Paprika, thyme, curry powder, bay leaves, bouillon powder, salt, and pepper: essential for flavoring the sauce..
Broth chicken, beef, or vegetable broth will work
Extras: You can also add some optional veggies like peas, sweet corn, and carrots for extra color and nutrition.
And if you want to make it a full meal, you can add some cooked protein of your choice, like diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu.

Procedure;

Typically, you prepare Jollof spaghetti by simmering the pasta in a flavourful, spicy tomato-based sauce similar to jollof rice.
You make the sauce with a blend of onion, tomato, pepper, and spices such as curry powder, thyme, and bay leaves.
Next, the spaghetti cooks in the sauce until completely absorbed, giving it a rich, bright red hue and richness.
Enjoy this versatile dish alone or serve it as a side dish with grilled or fried chicken, beef, or fish.

Chicken Fricassee

Chicken Fricassee is a traditional French delicacy featuring browned chicken simmered with herbs, onions, garlic, hearty vegetables.

INGREDIENTS DETAILS
Chicken: Preferably chicken thighs bone-in and skin-on. The browned chicken skin will turn out crispy and crusty. You can also use chicken breasts if you prefer.
Salt and Black pepper: to season the chicken and sauce.
Unsalted butter: French cuisine without butter is almost incomplete.
White mushrooms: this hearty vegetable is a game-changer in this recipe. You can also use button mushrooms or baby Bella.
Garlic and onions Why not?
Bay leaf and Thyme sprigs: For bringing out the flavor and freshness in the recipe.
All-purpose flour: Flour is the secret for thickening the sauce if you want a silky sauce.
Parsley: to garnish and add that finishing touch. It is optional.

Procedure;

  1. Season your chicken with salt and pepper – Use paper towels to dry your chicken, then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  2. Brown thighs by adding butter to a large skillet or pot and heat over medium-high heat until melted.
    Add chicken thighs, skin side down, and cook until golden brown, about 4 minutes. Flip and cook for 1 minute more, then remove to a plate.
  3. Sauté mushrooms, onion, carrots, and celery. bay leaves, and thyme for 5 minutes until lightly golden.
  4. Add garlic and stir for 30 seconds
  5. Add flour and leave the mixture to cook for 1 minute.
  6. Pour wine and chicken stock – Stir, scraping the pot’s base to dissolve the brown residue stuck to the pan.
  7. Add cream to the sauce and stir.
  8. Add the browned thighs and drumsticks back into the sauce with the skin side up.
  9. Cover with a lid and simmer under medium-low heat for about 10 minutes.
  10. Remove the lid and let it continue simmering for 20 minutes. This will allow the Chicken to cook well.
  11. Sprinkle with parsley, and serve!
